## 4. Observations (non-blocking)

- **O1 — Condo-semantics citation points at the wrong research section.** The packet, the connector docstrings, and the manifest all cite "research section 2.5" for the condo unit/billing lot-range semantics; that content actually lives in section 2.4 (meta_mappluto.pdf: unit 1001–6999 / billing 7501–7599, FKA merge) and section 4.4 (README one-record-per-complex). The mis-pointer originates in the packet itself and the underlying semantics are correctly grounded in the G1-verified official PDF, so this is a documentation-precision note, not a data error. Worth a one-line fix whenever these files are next touched.
- **O2 — The 20 ft tolerance is borrowed from the zoning-features (nyzd) accuracy statement**, not from MapPLUTO's own metadata (which states no numeric accuracy on the ArcGIS side). The producer discloses this honestly (report section 11.2) and the packet directed it; the borrow is conservative and visible in every classification output. Acceptable; a MapPLUTO-specific accuracy statement from the bulk `meta_mappluto.pdf` could tighten this later.
- **O3 — Real cross-channel area divergence found during my cross-check:** PLUTO SODA `lotarea` 91,351 (PTS-derived) vs ArcGIS `Shape__Area` 97,113.69 (DTM geometric) for BBL 1008350041. Both official; not this connector's concern (it cross-checks only its own computed area vs `Shape__Area`, which matched), but the future conflict engine must expect LotArea-vs-geometric-area disagreement as a normal condition.
- **O4 — Minor dead condition:** in `fetch_lot_geometry` the multiple-outcome guard `len(pairs) > 1 or (len(pairs) == MAX_FEATURES_PER_LOT and exceeded)` has a redundant second clause (10 > 1 already). Also the theoretical 1-feature-page-with-`exceededTransferLimit` case would classify as `single_feature` while preserving the flag visibly on the result. Cosmetic; behavior stays honest.
- **O5 — Cosmetic docstring artifact:** `analyze_lot_geometry`'s docstring contains a literal `{MAX_REPAIR_AREA_REL_DRIFT:.0%}` placeholder (plain docstring, never interpolated). No behavioral effect.
- **O6 — MPG02's "Empire State Building" purpose label:** the official PLUTO address for 1008350041 is "338 5 AVENUE" (the merged lot spans addresses). Block 835 / lot 41 is the commonly cited ESB lot; the nickname is descriptive only, and no data claim depends on it.
